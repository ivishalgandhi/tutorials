Go Pointers: When to Point, When to Copy

Build a solid mental model of Go pointers by comparing the same real-world feature with and without them. Lesson 1 walks through a task-status updater that silently fails when passed by value and succeeds when passed by pointer. You will learn the three pointer symbols, pointer receivers, and a simple decision table for choosing *T over T.
